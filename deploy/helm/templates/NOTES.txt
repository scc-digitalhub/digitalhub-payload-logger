1. The payload-logger gRPC service is now running.

To test the service, you can port-forward to access it locally:

  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "payload-logger.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward $POD_NAME 50051:50051

The service will be available at localhost:50051 for gRPC connections.
